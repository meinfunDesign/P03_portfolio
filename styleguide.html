Project analysis- moodboard experimental video<

Role: Ideation, storyboarding, location scheduling, video editing, special effects, music remixing

Tools: ​ After Effects 2017, Adobe Illustrator 2017, Canon T3i DSLRs, video sliver tripod (Manfrotto 128) ​ Location ​ Green room at Simon Fraser University Surrey Campus

Description: ​ Haziness is a visual effect video that includes a sense of "a spirit of haziness". Since I always have a hazy notion towards things when I am lack of sleep and stress during the final week of the semester. I want to share this experience and transform my mind activity into visible moving imagery. ​ The video focuses on the character movements, such as jumping, dancing and waving hands in the air to represent the emotion of having a hazy mind. The technique of using funky, high saturated, and symmetry styles (Kaleida effect in After effect) through the whole video, instead of a smoky environment, makes the video unique and creative.

Project analysis- process of experimental video

Process Analysis of Final Video: Glitch effect -> Spilt effect -> Transition to blue background -> Kaleida effects ​ For the jumping movement, I had zoom in the shots to focus on the legs and use the scale tool to mirror the character(me). Then, for the kaleida effects, I applied the flower, Can Maes, Wheel, Flippers, Unfold, and Dia cross effect with some a few rotations in order to establish the hazy mood.
