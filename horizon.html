Character sketches

THE HERO / PROTAGONIST

Name : Birdy/Milo

Species : The combination of duck, frog, and dinosaur-like creature.

Introduction : He is one of the brilliant and humble ones among the friend group. He thinks friendship is very important and would always be the one who fixes the problem and make peace among each other.

Backstory : Birdy was brought up in a warm and happy family until the death of his younger brother who was poisoned by molly flowers. His parents would always argue over little things, and he no longer feels the happiness he used to feel in the family. As a big brother, Birdy misses his little brother and would always be the one who finds the solution to solve the unhappy stuff among his group of friends. He feels the need to protect the people around him and always keep them happy. With a strong positive thinking, Birdy manages to live his life brightly, being looked up as an influential model by his friends. Despite the arguments between his parents, the tragic loss and unforgettable memory of his brother’s death, he still moves forward and gives unconditional love to the people around him.

Specialty : - Bright in critical thinking, good at solving clues but bad at direction. - Learn a bit of Krav Maga from their school and protect himself while facing a dangerous situation.

Additional Information: His strong positivity is the key to peace and happiness (hint: positive energy can defeat Momo’s evil influent towards Sanny and to cheer/coax with Sanny.)

THE FRIENDS

Name : Tinko

Species : The combination of snail and turtle.

Introduction : He is quiet and dozy most of the time. He has a faith to trust his friends and believes in logic-thinking and science. However, when it comes to a decision between life and friends, he would prefer to stay in the lab and do research.

Backstory : Tinko was brought up in a small cabin in the woods. He has 10 other siblings, he was born in the middle. His family is very familiar with the woods as they have stayed there for many decades. They are the well-known hunters of the woods. Despite their slow actions, their hunting techniques and survival skills are very strong. Because of the hybrid species between snail and turtle (the slowest animals), they are naturally dozy and sluggish in action, which can cause massive amnesia when they grow older. As Tinko’s grandfather grew older, Tinko realised that he is unable to recognised anybody from the family, the neighbours, and his friends. Tinko did not want to become like him, despite the amnesia runs in their species, he still pursues his study in science in order to cure memory loss.

Specialty : Used to live in the wood with his grandparents, he tends to know the forest well, has a good sense of direction. - While his wildness survival skills from his hunter family can help to leave clues to the survivor. - Not only that, he is good at researching and thinking logically.

Additional Information: His understanding in science and research experiences can help his friends when they are in danger or fallen sick/ poison.

A Shared Vision

Storyboard

As it is SFU’s vision to engage students, I was responsible for establish the story environment to engage the audience in the game experience. but more importantly to reach personal success for my second contest.

Reflection

I am grateful that this position has offered me a lot of creative direction and many opportunities to grow and reinforce relevant skills. Design can at times operate in their own separate silos, as I have experienced previously having taken up both roles separately (read more about it here). As a character designer, I was able to take command of both ends and realize the close coordination required to fulfill two goals simultaneously: the design immersion to increase the number of story experience, and the visual goal to motivate audience to keep playing the game. As I seek to pursue more design roles, this experience has made me more mindful of every design intent and the bigger picture behind it.
